Greeting Cards

Professionally printed using my own art and designs, Sweet Ingenuity cards are then cut and folded by hand. Each one measure approximately 4.75 inches by 4.75 inches, with some minor variation due to their handmade nature. They're printed on sturdy cardstock, and are blank inside. The back features my logo and website, and the text "handmade in Ottawa, Canada by a real live human". Each card comes with a simple 5 inch by 5 inch ungummed white envelope.

About the Artist

I'm an author, freelance illustrator, and all-around geek. I've been drawing and telling stories since before I can remember, and I'm not always sure where to draw the line between them. The stuff I make has always been eclectic, I love to learn and experiment with new methods and styles. I love cute things, but I'm also inspired by Victorian fashion and high fantasy, as well as modern pop culture. I like sticking things together in new ways, creating something new out of the juxtaposition. While I love drawing almost anything, I specialize in fantasy illustration.
